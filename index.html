Polène Flagship Store

Rather than a conventional retail interior, the Polène Flagship Store is conceived as an environment to be felt as much as seen – a place where material, light, and proportion come together to create a calm, tactile landscape.

The Fjord House

Set on the shores of a quiet fjord, this residence balances simple geometries with raw, natural materials, establishing a deep connection between the indoor living space and the surrounding wild landscape.
